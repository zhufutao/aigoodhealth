# Product

## Register

product

## Users

The primary user is a solo technical operator building and running a health recipe content workflow for WeChat public account and Xiaohongshu. They work in an authenticated admin console, moving between material capture, authoritative source review, AI structuring, topic selection, content packages, risk review, image assets, and manual publishing metrics.

## Product Purpose

The product turns scattered human inspiration and authoritative health sources into conservative, reviewable content drafts. Success means the operator can trace each generated topic and content package back to structured material, review risk language, copy publishing-ready text, download visual assets, and record post-publishing performance without losing context.

## Brand Personality

Calm, editorial, trustworthy. The interface should feel like a serious content operations desk for a health editor: precise, legible, and quietly confident.

## Anti-references

Avoid consumer wellness decoration, generic AI gradients, medical-tech coldness, overbuilt marketing dashboards, playful illustration-heavy screens, and noisy card grids that make operational data hard to scan.

## Design Principles

1. Make the workflow visible: each screen should make the next action obvious.
2. Separate evidence from inspiration: authoritative material and manual inspiration should feel clearly classified.
3. Prefer operational clarity over decoration: dense information is welcome when hierarchy is disciplined.
4. Keep health-risk language conservative: UI should reinforce review, caution, and traceability.
5. Optimize for copying, editing, and checking: the console is a working desk, not a showcase.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Aim for WCAG AA contrast, visible keyboard focus, reduced-motion support, readable Chinese text at operational densities, and touch targets that remain usable on tablet-width screens.
